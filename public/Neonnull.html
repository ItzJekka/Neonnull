ABOUT THE GAME

Neon Null is a high-velocity 2D roguelite set deep in space, where cyberpunk technology collides with hostile cosmic geometry. Each run reshapes your build. Branching upgrades reshape how you move and fight, builds evolve rapidly, and failure fuels mastery.

Neon Null grows louder, stranger, and more reactive with every update.

◆ PROCEDURAL CHAOS

■ SHAPE MASTERY

Destroy and absorb hostile shapes to mutate your abilities and create personalized builds

● COSMIC PROGRESSION

Unlock upgrades and ship modifications that push each attempt further than the last..until the signal drops.
