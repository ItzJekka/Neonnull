Patch Notes Pre-Alpha V0.37

Release Date: April 23, 2026

Welcome to the latest patch notes for Neon Null! In this update, we've made significant improvements to the game's performance and stability, as well as added new features and content. Here's a breakdown of what's new:

  • New:
    • Added a new upgrade that increases the chance to find a cosmic gem.
  • Changes:
    • Ammo for offensive and utility modules now recharges after a boss fight or a completed challenge.
    • Adjusted pacer triangle teleportation logic and visual effects.
    • Adjusted circle swarm logic, they were not supposed to be wired to be aggressive when followers were present and that has now been fixed.
    • Added an animation transition to circle swarm leaders' throw ability.
    • Lowered the spawn rate of circle swarms.
    • Major enemy spawning formula adjustments.
    • Major adjustments to cosmic pulse. It no longer pulses every 15 seconds, instead it is now a usable ability like phase shift (double tap boost).
    • Removed the phase shift hud icon on the cursor, it now uses the same indicator as cosmic pulse (ring inside ship).
    • Adjusted the rogue signal enemy's zone ability.
    • Renamed 'Upgrade Rarity' to 'Rarity Boost' and correctly adjusted its numbers to reflect what it actually provides in terms of boosting rarity.
    • The 'Scarred Dogtags' memento is now selected by default for new players.
    • Increased delay of rogue signal's teleport attack slightly.
    • Increased floating combat text size across the board by 15%.
  • Fixes:
    • Fixed a bug where players could phase shift or decoy beacon stealth through ciradion the endless' walls.
    • Fixed a bug where the phase shift HUD element was enabled even when the player didn't have the upgrade.
    • Fixed MANY edge cases where neon rain did not properly effect certain boss damage or player damage.
    • Updated singularity sledge's description to more accurately reflect what it is.
    • Updated cosmic pulse's description to more accurately reflect what it is.
    • Fixed a bug where rogue signal's zone visual effects would not disappear sometimes after dying.
    • Fixed a bug where homing rockets would sometimes not find a target or even attempt to home.
    • Various hangar tweaks.

Thank you for playing Neon Null! We hope you enjoy the new content and improvements. As always, we welcome your feedback and suggestions for future updates.
