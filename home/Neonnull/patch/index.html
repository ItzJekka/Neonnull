Patch Notes Pre-Alpha V0.35

Release Date: April 14, 2026

Welcome to the latest patch notes for Neon Null! In this update, we've made significant improvements to the game's performance and stability, as well as added new features and content. Here's a breakdown of what's new:

  • New:
    • Added several new uncommon / common upgrades for Decoy Beacon.
    • Added a brand new event possibility, the 'Rogue Signal'.
  • Changes:
    • Changed the color of several enemies.
    • Adjusted the values of the new neon lance upgrades to make them a more interesting pick.
    • Added a boss bullet range increase for the pentagon, square and rhombus bosses.
    • Removed the flashbang-esque phase transition effect from the celestial twins phase transitions & cosmic titan wormhole transitions.
    • Adjusted dragon boss music volume to make it significantly less loud.
    • Added a compressor to audio FX chain to eliminate crackles from stacked sound (like multiple rocket impacts for example) as well as fix other audio issues.
    • Removed the shooting stars from the celestial twins boss fight to reduce the amount of clutter.
    • Changed the layout of the Data Matrix nodes.
    • Reworked the thruster exhaust on all three ship drives.
    • Added a subtle telegraph to the blue triangle shooter enemy.
    • Tweaked the star enemy's telegraph animation a little bit.
  • Fixes:
    • Fixed a bug with the rarity color bars in the upgrade menu being incorrect.
    • Fixed a bug that was causing decoy beacon damage to not hit certain bosses.
    • Fixed a big bug with Comeback Protocol.
    • Fixed the celestial twins boss fight soundtrack not playing when the fight started.
    • Fixed a bug that was causing boss badges to reappear on the next boss fight in certain scenarios.
    • Fixed missing chrono stutter references causing certain enemies during certain instances to not be effected.
    • Many rhombus boss fight tweaks, fixes and changes.
    • Fixed a few small things with the golden pacer triangles.
    • Fixed a bug where the background lightning for the dragon boss would show up in the upgrade menu before the boss spawned.

Thank you for playing Neon Null! We hope you enjoy the new content and improvements. As always, we welcome your feedback and suggestions for future updates.
