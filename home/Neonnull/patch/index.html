Patch Notes Pre-Alpha V0.40

Release Date: June 7th, 2026

Welcome to the latest patch notes for Neon Null! V0.40 is now live, and we're excited to share all the new content, changes, and fixes that we've implemented in this update. This also includes Windows,Mac AND Linux / SteamOS support.

  • New:
    • Added a new 'empower' ability to pacer triangles that provides a shield and movement speed buff to enemies.
    • Added a new enemy, the ring.
    • Added a subtle breathing effect to the ship's lights.
    • Added a reactive effect to the ship's lights when boosting.
    • Added a new background visual effect.
    • Added a new tech upgrade, 'IFF Spoof'.
    • Added new icons for all mementos. Still technically placeholders.
    • Added new icons and assets for all of the hulls.
    • Added a toggle in the settings to hide the version watermark, as well as a toggle for the system stability UI element.
  • Changes:
    • Added a floating damage number for friendly fire damage done by enemies.
    • Citadel Null's projectiles can now damage the bit turrets it spawns, and the bit turret's projectiles can now damage Citadel Null as well.
    • Adjusted the color on pacer triangles and cross enemies to fit in a little bit better.
    • Made some tweaks to the way background effects are handled to better space them out and handle their recycling better.
    • Citadel Null's split phase now varies in length by a little bit.
    • Adjusted spawning logic for various enemies for better pacing, especially in the early game.
    • Adjusted the drive upgrades to not just be incremental upgrades. Now they have their own identity and playstyle.
    • Adjusted the amount of fire rate given by upgrades to make it less of a meta pick.
    • Very slightly nerfed Pyroneon Rounds and Energy shield.
    • Changed the telegraph for neon lance from an arrow to a sort of 'lane' visual effect -- which looks and feels better.
    • Updated the visual effects for neon lance. It was one of the earliest features in the game and was due for one.
    • More changes and improvements to text in various parts of the game.
    • Buffed the health on the rhombus enemy.
    • Adjusted system overload despawn logic to better distribute deletion of enemies before the boss fight starts.
  • Fixes:
    • Optimized shader handling to reduce the possibility for shader related errors.
    • Optimized gem and health bubble pooling, which should improve FPS.
    • Optimized steering / avoidance logic on enemies, which should improve FPS.
    • Fixed 'Follow-Through' upgrade being offered without the proper pre-requisites.
    • Made a few optimizations / improvements to pacer triangle teleportation.
    • Fixed a bug where rocket damage would not register if the rocket did not actually hit an enemy (i.e. if it timed out and exploded before hitting an enemy, but an enemy was still in the explosion radius).
    • Challenge panels are now hidden when in the upgrade menu.

Thank you for playing Neon Null! We hope you enjoy the new content and improvements. As always, we welcome your feedback and suggestions for future updates.
