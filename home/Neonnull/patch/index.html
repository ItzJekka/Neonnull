Patch Notes Pre-Alpha V0.32

Release Date: March 24, 2026

Welcome to the latest patch notes for Neon Null! In this update, we've made significant improvements to the game's performance and stability, as well as added new features and content. Here's a breakdown of what's new:

  • New:
    • Added the Data Matrix. You can spend data that you accumulate in-game to roll for randomized upgrades, then place them in your data matrix to apply them to your runs.
    • Added the cosmic titan, a new recurring event that occurs at least once every playthrough depending on how far you get. This is still VERY much in the works.
    • Added a new, additional first boss. There will be a 50% chance to get this boss OR the pentagon at level 10.
    • Added a new enemy to the mix, the cross.
    • Revamped the standard triangle enemy's overall logic. This enemy didn't really work well, and given that it was one of the most common enemies, that was a huge issue that I've wanted to tackle. More tweaks to come to this.
    • I very drastically increased prediction logic. It is MUCH better now, like, it is now at almost flawless accuracy.
    • Added names and title text above the health bar for all bosses.
    • Added a new cosmic+ upgrade, 'Sanguine Overflow'.
    • Added a new tech upgrade, 'Thermal Exhaust'.
    • Added a new tech upgrade, 'Surge Collector'.
    • Added two new songs to the soundtrack.
  • Changes:
    • Did a pass on the prestige progress panel to make it nicer / less flat looking. Still far from finished.
    • Added upgrade rarity as a stat in the hangar menu.
    • Added regular, elite, and boss damage as a stat in the hangar menu.
    • Added two decimal places to the stats panel, since it is needed now.
    • Overhauled the rocket impact's visual FX.
    • Buffed 'Vampiric Rounds' from 0.10% to 0.35%.
    • Improved environmental lighting.
    • Increased the healing amount received from corrupted boss mechanics (mechanics that are green) from 1 to 2.
    • Increased the healing frequency of the corrupted beam on the circle boss.
    • Slowed down the rate at which cosmic vacuum pulls gems, to make it a little more satisfying.
    • Increased the recharge time of Decoy Beacon slightly (was still way too strong / powerful / easy to use).
    • Gave an initial pass to the game over screen.
    • Decreased the size of the status cluster bar at the top left slightly, and repositioned it slightly.
  • Fixes:
    • Fixed a number of scaling issues in the hangar panel for users on ultra wide monitors.
    • Fixed plasma surge protocol not working, since boosting was disabled by default it didn't actually work or do anything. Now, if you have Plasma Surge Protocol, you are allowed to boost while using plasma beam & it gives the bonus it was intended to give.

Thank you for playing Neon Null! We hope you enjoy the new content and improvements. As always, we welcome your feedback and suggestions for future updates.
